03. Scope of job

1. Strategic Concept Planning

1.1. Research & Strategic Framework

  • Preliminary research and clarification talk with the client
  • Definition of project goals, target audience, and desired positioning
  • Context research: local and regional conditions, competitive environment, cultural specifics, etc.

1.2. Concept Development

  • Development of an innovative, commercially viable, and operationally sound SPA concept
  • Preparation of preliminary treatment and function specifications for all spaces
  • Definition of treatment types, service sequence, average capacity, and key operational needs
  • Alignment between treatment logic, guest comfort, and back-of-house functionality
  • Zoning and guest journey mapping — development of a functional layout that defines how guests and staff move through the space, including subdivision into all main and technical areas (treatment rooms, wet zone, storage, preparation, and staff areas), presented in clear diagram form
  • In collaboration with the project’s architects, we will refine the layout through several iterations to ensure optimal spatial solutions, functionality, and guest flow
  • Development of a structured strategic brief that bridges vision and implementation, turning conceptual intent into a clear foundation for design and operation

Deliverable (Stage 1)

  • Strategic SPA concept document (PDF)
  • Zoning and functional layout schemes (PLA/DWG + PDF)

2. Schematic Design

Translating the approved concept into a tangible spatial vision — defining how every area of the spa functions, feels, and connects. This stage includes:

  • Master Plan Layout — development of the complete functional plan for all spa areas, detailing guest and staff zones, treatment rooms, wet areas, and back-of-house spaces
  • Coordination and refinement of the layout in collaboration with the client and project architects; up to two revisions included
  • Guest and Staff Flow Diagram — visualization of movement and operational logic throughout the facility, ensuring seamless guest experience and efficient service circulation
  • Calculation of wellness capacity and throughput — determining the optimal number of guests and treatment sessions per shift and per day, based on spatial parameters and service logic
  • Preliminary load calculations for the leading technological and wellness equipment to support further engineering design

Deliverable (Stage 2)

  • Detailed master plan layout (PLA/DWG + PDF)
  • Capacity calculation (PDF)
  • Load calculation (Excel)

The Result

A concept that is thoughtful, evidence-based, and ready for the next design phase — one that feels natural, restorative, and efficient in both guest experience and operations.
